Immunogenicity Experience

Immunogenicity and technologist leader with over a decade of experience spanning biologics discovery, preclinical development and biotech platform strategy.

Internationally recognized for expertise in immunogenicity risk assessment and mitigation. Extensive experience operating at the interface of immunology, protein engineering, bioanalytics, and computational science to proactively de-risk biologic programs and enable confident development decisions.

  • Immunogenicity risk assessment, mitigation, and strategy
  • Translational immunology and immune tolerance
  • In silico, in vitro, and in vivo immunogenicity tools
  • AI/ML and agentic workflows in biologics R&D

Past Experience

I have held positions of increasing responsibility in biologics development organizations, driving strategy and building functions focused on minimizing risk.

  • Director, Head of Immunogenicity at Xaira Therapeutics
  • Associate Director, Predictive & Translational Biology at Generate Biomedicines
  • Led cross-functional strategy spanning technology development and pipeline drug development programs

Education

  • PhD, Cancer Biology (Immunology Focus) - University of Chicago
  • MS, Translational Sciences - University of Chicago
