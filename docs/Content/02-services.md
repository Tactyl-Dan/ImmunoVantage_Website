## COMMON SERVICES & OFFERINGS

**Section Title:** Common Services & Offerings

### Service 1: Discovery to IND Support
<!-- ID: discovery-to-ind -->

**Description:** Immunogenicity guidance from the earliest stages of discovery through IND submission.

**Features:**
- In silico immunogenicity prediction results review
- Drug target, disease and therapeutic modality risk evaluation
- IND-enabling risk assessment studies design
- Drafting immunogenicity risk sections of IND 

### Service 2: Risk Mitigation Planning
<!-- ID: risk-mitigation -->

**Description:** Proactive risk identification with actionable mitigation strategies customized to your specific program's context.

**Features:**
- Review of target compound and target product profiles
- Risk factor identification and scoring
- Mitigation strategy development
- Clinical context considerations


### Service 3: Program Troubleshooting
<!-- ID: troubleshooting -->

**Description:** Rapid assessment and strategic recommendations for programs facing immunogenicity challenges.

**Features:**
- Unexpected ADA signal investigation
- Regulatory response preparation
- Root cause analysis support
- Risk-benefit assessment

### Service 4: Clinical Immunogenicity Strategy
<!-- ID: clinical-strategy -->

**Description:** Development of comprehensive clinical immunogenicity testing strategies and regulatory-aligned protocols.

**Features:**
- Clinical ADA testing strategy
- Sample collection schedule design
- Integrated summary of immunogenicity (ISI) preparation
- Clinical impact correlation analysis

### Service 5: Assay Development Guidance & Validation
<!-- ID: assay-oversight -->

**Description:** Technical guidance for nonclinical immunogenicity risk assessment and clinical immune monitoring assays.

**Features:**
- CRO selection and coordination
- Nonclinical assay development and validation
- ADA assay development review
- Drug tolerance and cut-point guidance

### Service 6: Comparability Assessments
<!-- ID: comparability -->

**Description:** Expert evaluation of immunogenicity risk implications from process changes, supporting your comparability exercises and regulatory submissions.

**Features:**
- Manufacturing change impact assessment
- Biosimilar immunogenicity strategy
- Critical quality attribute evaluation
- Regulatory submission support
